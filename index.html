My perfect vacation

By Laksh Dandona

Switzerland

A landlocked country of towering mountains, deep Alpine lakes, grassy valleys dotted with neat farms and small villages, and thriving cities that blend the old and the new, Switzerland is the nexus of the diverse physical and cultural geography of western Europe, renowned for both its natural beauty and its way of life.

The main reason why Switzerland is the perfect vacation for me are the Alps. Hiking mountains is my favorite hobby, and this small but rugged country has the best mountains and peaks. These amazing mountains are also home to the world's top ski resorts.

CHOCOLATES. I love chocolates so much, and Swiss chocolates are world class. Switzerland is arguably best known for its milk chocolate, with milk chocolate making up 80% of all chocolate consumed in Switzerland, and for good reason! Swiss chocolate contains more milk than most chocolate, offering a distinctly smooth and creamy flavour and texture you won't find elsewhere.
